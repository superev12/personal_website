## Overview

NoteTamer does not collect or store any data of any kind.

Effective Date: 2026-01-20

## 1. No Data Collection
We do not collect any personal information about you. This includes, but is not limited to:

    Names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
    Location data (GPS).
    Device identifiers (IMEI, IDFA).
    Usage statistics or analytics.

## 2. No Data Storage

As we do not collect any data, we do not store any data on our servers. Any information you input into the app stays strictly on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## 3. No Third-Party Tracking

NoteTamer does not use third-party analytics or advertising networks.

## 4. Permissions

This app does not require any special permissions.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy in the future.
